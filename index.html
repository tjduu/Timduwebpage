ESG data analyst

Beginning my career as an ESG Sentinel Analyst at Elevate Global in Hong Kong, I used AI to effectively monitor real-time ESG incidents within supply chains. My portfolio spans a range of projects in data science, climate change, and sustainability - each one further building my understanding and expertise. About Me
